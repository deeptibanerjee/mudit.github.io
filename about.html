Meet Mudit, a vibrant singer, songwriter, and producer who has been creating music and performing it across Asia, and North America in the indie, soul, and progressive music genres. Born and raised in Delhi, the heart of India.


Mudit was exposed to music and production very late in his life at the age of 23 but he kept going forward with immense curiosity to learn and excel as a musician. He started playing guitar in his early music career and piano lately in his 12 years of musical journey.


He was drawn to the soulful sounds of artists like Chris Martin from Coldplay band, Noel Gallagher from Oasis band, Bonobo, and Steven Wilson from Porcupine Tree band. He also got his inspiration from the theatrical and cinematic music of A.R. Rahman and Hans Zimmer.
